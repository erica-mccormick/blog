Let's Talk (Earth) Science!

Hi, I'm Erica! (she/her) I am a full-time research assistant in the Rempe Vadose Zone Lab at the Jackson School of Geosciences at the University of Texas at Austin. My current research is on the importance of rock moisture (bedrock water storage in the unsaturated zone) as a resource for plants! When I'm not actively *doing* science, I love to talk and write about it! Check back here for research updates on cool new work I'm reading about, tips and tutorials (especially Google Earth Engine), and stories from talking to my friends and colleagues about what's happening in their research worlds! If you would like to contribute to this blog or have something exciting to talk about, don't hesitate to reach out!
